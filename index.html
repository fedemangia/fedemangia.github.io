My digital projects

Process automation

RPA program developed using VBA in Excel to automate several operative tasks performed in IBM Host-on-Demand mainframe 3270, reducing execution time for Purchasing and Logistics teams on more than 50%.


Example 1

In this video, the program extracts 264 pieces of information from mainframe and puts them organized in an Excel sheet in less than a minute. Before this project, the information was being copied and pasted manually one by one.


Example 2

In this video, after the user analysis and inputs in Excel, the program reads the information from Excel and replicates it in the mainframe automatically.


Example 3

In this video, after extracting information from the mainframe, the program allows the user to visualize useful data in a fast and easy way.


I created this project while I was working as a Buyer in the Indirect Purchasing team at Ford Motor Company. At this time, me and other 7 Buyers where handling the purchasing of almost 30.000 items with no other tool than the IBM HOD mainframe, dedicating most of our time to run manual and repetitive operative tasks with no value-added. I created this project without being asked to do it, just motivated by my process-improving mindset. Some of the results after implementing this project:

  • It reduced work execution time for Purchasing and Logistics team on more than 50%.
  • It improved employees satisfaction.
  • It reduced extra-time works.
  • It allowed employes to focus on more strategic and value-added work.
  • It avoided employees health issues, such as: high stress, burn-out syndrome and ergonomic illnesses.
